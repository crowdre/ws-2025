The 9th International Workshop on Crowd-Based Requirements Engineering (CrowdRE'25)



September 2, 2025
Held in Valencia, Spain
In conjunction with RE 2025

Program

CrowdRE'25 is co-located with the RE 2025 conference. The workshop intends to promote lively discussions among participants from both industry and academia.

The paper session in the below program includes 25-minute slots (15-minute presentation, and 10-minute discussion) for each paper. The presenter of each paper is underlined.

Time Session
09:30–10:30 Welcome and Keynote: "From the Crowd to Agents? Exploring the Role of Agentic AI in Crowd Requirements Engineering" by Andreas Vogelsang
Break
11:00–11:30 P1: Towards Better Requirements from the Crowd: Developer Engagement with Feature Requests in Open Source Software
Pragyan K C, Mitra Bokaei Hosseini, Thomas Herron, Rambod Ghandiparsi and John Heaps.
Discussant: Marc Herrmann
11:30–12:00 P2: From App Features to Explanation Needs: Analyzing Correlations and Predictive Potential
Martin Obaidi, Kushtrim Qengaj, Jakob Droste, Hannah Deters, Marc Herrmann, Jil Klünder, Elisa Schmid and Kurt Schneider.
Discussant: Pragyan K C
12:00–12:30 P3: Towards Using Personas in Requirements Engineering: What Has Been Changed Recently?
Chowdhury Shahriar Muzammel, Maria Spichkova and James Harland.
Discussant: Elisa Schmid
Lunch
14:00–14:30 P4: A Gold-Standard German Dataset for Sentiment Analysis in Software Engineering
Martin Obaidi, Marc Herrmann, Elisa Schmid, Raymond Ochsner, Kurt Schneider and Jil Klünder.
Discussant: Chowdhury Shahriar Muzammel
14:30–15:30 Interactive session
Break
16:00–17:00 Keynote: "The Crowd is Rooting for Future Proofing" By Birgit Penzenstadler
17:00–17:30 Closing by organisers

Note: This program is tentative and subject to change.
